How It Works

A five-step workflow built for agency service teams

01

Incoming emails are captured

New service emails are monitored directly from the shared inbox as they arrive.

02

Sensitive requests are screened

Higher-risk emails are identified and blocked before reaching AI. They are routed immediately for human review.

03

Safe requests are organized

Routine requests are categorized and logged into a structured queue - no more manual sorting.

04

The team gets a clear operational queue

Staff see what came in, what needs review, what was blocked, and what has a draft ready - all in one place.

05

Drafts are prepared, not sent

A draft acknowledgment is created for staff review. Nothing goes out without a person approving it first.
